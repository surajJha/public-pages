🕉️ Gitasaar — Privacy Policy

Your companion for the Bhagavad Gita

com.gitasaar

Last Updated: May 2026

Summary: Gitasaar is an offline-first app. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Everything you create — conversations, bookmarks, journals, reflections — stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

1. Overview

Gitasaar ("we", "our", "the app") is a Bhagavad Gita companion app built by Arya Tech. This Privacy Policy describes what data the app handles, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

By using Gitasaar, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Gitasaar does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on external servers:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to third parties
  • Voice recordings or audio files

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you generate in Gitasaar is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Share feature.

  • Conversations: Chat messages with the on-device AI assistant
  • Bookmarks: Verses you have saved for reference
  • Reflections & Journal: Personal notes and reflections
  • Journey Progress: Your progress through guided learning paths
  • Streaks: Daily practice activity tracking
  • Preferences: Theme, language, and notification settings
  • Quiz History: Quiz scores and progress

You can delete all local data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

4. App Permissions

Gitasaar requests the following Android permissions:

RECORD_AUDIO
Voice input to the AI assistant and verse recitation practice. Audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
INTERNET
Used only for the one-time download of the on-device AI model (~500MB–1GB from HuggingFace). No personal data is transmitted.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Keeps the AI model inference running while you interact with the assistant, without the OS terminating the process mid-response.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Sends local reminders for daily practice streaks and model download progress. All notifications are generated on-device with no external server involved.

5. On-Device AI Processing

Gitasaar uses an on-device language model (Google Gemma via MediaPipe) for the AI chat feature.

  • The model runs entirely on your device — no cloud API is called
  • Your questions and the AI's responses are never sent to any server
  • The model requires a one-time download; after that the app works fully offline

6. Internet Usage

The app uses an internet connection only for:

  • Model download: One-time download of the AI language model

No analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or telemetry data is ever transmitted over the internet. After the model download is complete, the app functions fully offline.

7. Third-Party Services

Gitasaar does not integrate any third-party analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel), advertising (e.g., AdMob), or social login services. The only external network request is the model download from HuggingFace, which is a one-time operation.

8. Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with, sold to, or rented to third parties under any circumstances.

When you use the Share feature (e.g., sharing a verse card or quote), the content is passed to your device's standard Android sharing sheet. Gitasaar has no control over or visibility into which app you choose to share to.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete it within the app (e.g., deleting a conversation or journal entry)
  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's data via Android Settings → Apps → Gitasaar → Clear Data

You can configure conversation retention policies in the app's Settings (options: keep all, 30 days, 90 days, recent 20, or recent 50).

10. Children's Privacy

Gitasaar does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no age-gated content and stores no personal information on external servers. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

11. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We do not transmit data to external servers, so there is no risk of server-side data breach affecting your Gitasaar data.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app's features or applicable law. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
